About me

First Steps

For 16 years, I devoted myself to the noble profession of teaching English. Throughout my teaching career, I continuously refined my skills in time management, communication effectiveness, and resilience. I embraced innovative teaching methods, always eager to explore new approaches and techniques to enhance the learning experience. I actively fostered a sense of responsibility and independence among my students, encouraging their engagement in problem-solving and collaborative tasks.

Transitioning Careers: Embracing a New Path

My transition into the field of Business Information Technology represents an exciting new chapter in my professional journey. It began with my curiosity for the IT field, which was further sparked after obtaining a certification in web design from NUCBA in July 2022. This pivotal moment set me on the path of becoming a student of Business Information Technology. I am eager to apply my passion for learning and innovation to software development and data science, contributing to innovative projects and driving positive change in the tech industry.

Forging Future Paths: My Career Aspirations

As I embark on my new journey in software development and data science, I am overflowing with excitement to apply all the knowledge and skills I've gained during my time at Red River College. I can't wait to bring my unique blend of experiences and talents to dynamic teams and innovative projects. My heart is set on using what I've learned to create websites that are not just functional, but also enjoyable to use. In college, I discovered a passion for front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, where I found joy in crafting user-friendly interfaces. On the back end, I've delved into the world of C#, developing applications that not only work smoothly but also engage users in meaningful ways. I'm eager to continue learning and growing in this field, contributing to projects that make a real difference in people's lives.
